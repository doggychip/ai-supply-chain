⚠ REIT Warning: Power Constraints Limiting Value Capture
Despite AI tailwinds, EQIX (−13%), DLR (−11%), and IRM (−16%) have underperformed vs S&P 500 over the past year. The market believes AI profit primarily accrues to chip and hyperscaler companies, not DC landlords. Power availability and access to speculative capital are the gating constraints. REITs suit income-focused portfolios; growth-focused AI portfolios may prefer chips/cloud.
